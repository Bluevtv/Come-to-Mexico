Why Mexico?

Mexico is the best getaway destination for its incredible food, and stunning landscapes ranging from Caribbean beaches to lush green jungles. Whether you want to explore ancient ruins or relax at a world-class resort, its warm hospitality with great food, affordability, and landscapes make it a perfect escape for any traveler.

  • World-Class Beaches
  • Extraordinary Food
  • Vibrant Festivals
  • Beautiful Landscapes
  • Adventurous Activities

Top Things to Do

Mexico offers a diverse mix of ancient Maya and Aztec ruins (Chichén Itzá, Teotihuacan), stunning Caribbean beaches, and vibrant urban culture in Mexico City, featuring world-class museums and street food. Top experiences include snorkeling in cenotes, whale watching, exploring colonial cities like Oaxaca, and touring vibrant local markets. Mexico has a great variety of activities and stunning landscapes

  1. Explore Chichén Itzá
  2. Relax on Isla Mujeres
  3. Visit Xcaret
  4. Food tour in Mexico City
  5. Snorkel in clear waters

Travel Tips

Tipping in Mexico is customary, with 10–20% expected at restaurants and 20–50 pesos for services like housekeeping or bellhops. Use pesos for better value, carry small bills, and tip daily. Safety requires staying aware, avoiding unlit areas at night, and using reputable transport. Basic Spanish is appreciated, and all-inclusive staff usually appreciate cash tips.

  • Use pesos
  • Drink purified water
  • Use safe transport
  • Learn basic Spanish
